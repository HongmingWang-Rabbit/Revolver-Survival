================================================================================
REVOLVER SURVIVAL - GAME DETAILS
================================================================================
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GAME DESCRIPTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Revolver Survival is a high-stakes survival game inspired by Russian Roulette.
Players choose their risk level by selecting how many bullets to load into a
6-chamber revolver, then pull the trigger. Survive and win multiplied rewards,
or face elimination.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GAME MODES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mode            Bullets    Survival Rate    Multiplier    RTP
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
1 Bullet        1          83.33%           1.17x         97.50%
2 Bullets       2          66.67%           1.46x         97.33%
3 Bullets       3          50.00%           1.95x         97.50%
4 Bullets       4          33.33%           2.93x         97.67%
5 Bullets       5          16.67%           5.86x         97.67%

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
MATHEMATICAL SPECIFICATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Chambers: 6
- House Edge: 2.33%
- RTP Range: 97.33% - 97.67%
- RTP Variation: 0.34% (within 0.5% requirement)
- Mode Cost: 1.0 (all modes)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
FEATURES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Double or Nothing: After surviving, players can continue betting their
  winnings for higher rewards or cash out
- Social Mode Support: Compliant terminology for Stake.us social gaming
- RGS Integration: Full Stake Engine RGS API integration
- Demo Mode: Fallback demo mode when RGS is unavailable
- Responsive Design: Mobile and desktop compatible
